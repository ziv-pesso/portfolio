Evertown

About

“Evertown” is set in Everwing’s universe. It is a Facebook Messenger game in which you get fancier buildings by merging similar buildings with each other. Once certain milestones are reached new building plots are awarded to help the player’s town grow.

The core loop of "Evertown" went through many iterations. The one shown on these mocks is the last iteration released for it. The idea was simplified from past versions to a UI that shows the player a sequence of rewards. Its purpose is to show the player something to continue playing towards. When I designed it I kept in mind that engineers should be able to easily animate it programmatically...

Play Game

Lightning Round Mini Game

This is a bonus minigame that becomes accessible to players when they reach the stopwatch icon on the “Reward Treadmill”. The player basically tries to get a high score by merging as many buildings as possible before time runs out. This breaks the main game routine by giving the players a sense of urgency. My main intention was to make the score and timer as big as possible, both making it very readable and also encouraging the player to hurry. The flow for these screens was heavily influenced by "Everwing".

Level Up Iterations

I was tasked with exploring the look of a player’s Level Up Screen. At this stage of production, there was interest in adding Everwing characters into the UI to tie Evertown with that game’s universe. To get a clearer direction I created a few variations that I then shared with my art director. Once a layout was chosen it would get polished and new assets would be sent to developers for implementation. The mechanics for the game changed a few times after this, so these iterations ended up being scrapped at the end.
