Clan Outposts

I designed the layout of the Clan Outpost shop and the Outpost buildings menu for Everwing. Also had the opportunity to illustrate some of it’s items. I wanted their containers to pop both in color and contrast from the background. People want store items to look shiny and feel enticing.

Skill Icons

These icons were used to symbolize skills that give special modifiers to player’s Fairies and Dragon sidekicks. We decided to assign colors based on their functionality or element (Freezing being blue for example). Some of these were also specific to a Fairy or Boss, so it was important for me to try and use either a motif, color or prop that connects with that character.

Backgrounds & Dragons

These are backgrounds I created that were used to show off the Dragons in the Dragon equip screen.

Promotional Art

The team at Gameclosure was pretty small and once in a while, I would get the opportunity to make marketing graphics. These were fun to create. I had to come up with ideas based on art that was already made for the game (The wonderful art team at Gameclosure created these assets). Since these are on social media, they compete with everything else being shown to the viewer. That is why I always pushed for a lot of color and contrast to catch people’s attention. I enjoyed working on them because I get to learn how other departments work and it challenges me creatively in a different way.
