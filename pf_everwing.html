Clan Outposts

I designed the layout of the Clan Outpost shop and the Outpost buildings menu for Everwing. Furthermore, I had the opportunity to illustrate some of it’s items. I wanted their containers to pop from the background both in color and contrast. Players want store items to look shiny and feel enticing.

Skill Icons

These icons were used to symbolize skills that give special modifiers to players' Fairies and Dragon sidekicks. We decided to assign colors based on their functionality or element (freezing being blue for example). Some of these were also specific to a Fairy or Boss, so it was important for me to try and use either a motif, color, or prop specific to that character.

Backgrounds & Dragons

These backgrounds I created were used to show off the Dragons in the Dragon equip screen.

Promotional Art

Once in a while, I would get the opportunity to make marketing graphics which were fun to create. I had to come up with ideas based on art that was already made for the game (these were created by the wonderful art team at Gameclosure). Since the graphics are used on social media, they compete with all other content shown to the viewer, so I always pushed for a lot of color and contrast to catch people’s attention. I enjoyed working on these graphics, because it challenged me creatively and gave me a chance to learn how other departments work.
