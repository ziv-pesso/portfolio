Character Reskin

Above are a few quick outfit iterations using an already established character. I decided to go with a Shakesperean theme for the Valentines edition of the game. Creating various versions helped both the team and I to figure out which elements and shapes we liked.

The screen above is the Texture Atlas template the animation team was using for this animation rig. While designing the outfit, I took into account the boundaries that they had set. On the right side is the final look of the character with all the parts put together.
